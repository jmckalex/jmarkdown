A column-span-only table:

\begin{tabular}{lll}
\hline
H1 & H2 & H3 \\
\hline
\multicolumn{3}{l}{This cell spans 3 columns} \\
\multicolumn{2}{l}{Two-col span} & Single \\
\hline
\end{tabular}

A row-span + col-span table:

\begin{tabular}{lrl}
\hline
\multicolumn{2}{l}{\multirow{2}{*}{This header spans two columns and two rows       }} & Header A \\
\multicolumn{2}{c}{} & Header B \\
\hline
Cell A & Cell B & Cell C \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
